# Model Provider Terms Flow-Down (Template)

Last updated: 2026-03-15

Purpose: ensure end users are contractually bound to restrictions required by
model providers used by this service.

## Required clauses for product Terms of Service

Include clauses that:

1. Bind users to comply with platform AUP and provider-imposed restrictions.
2. Prohibit attempts to disable or evade safety systems.
3. Permit suspension/termination for violations.
4. State that generated content may be filtered or blocked for safety/legal
   compliance.
5. Reference third-party model terms where required.

## Operational flow-down requirements

- Product/legal pages link to current AUP and third-party model terms.
- Enforcement actions are logged for auditability.
- Security and trust teams can rapidly respond to abuse reports.

## Gemma-specific mapping

- Gemma Terms and policy docs must be reflected in:
  - customer-facing ToS
  - AUP
  - internal moderation and incident runbooks

## Integration checklist

- [ ] ToS includes model-provider restrictions clause
- [ ] AUP includes prohibited-use categories
- [ ] Moderation pipeline active for input/output
- [ ] Appeals/reporting process documented
- [ ] Periodic policy refresh process assigned
