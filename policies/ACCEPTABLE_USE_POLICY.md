# Acceptable Use Policy (Template)

Last updated: 2026-03-15

This policy governs use of the language tutor platform.

## Allowed use

- Language learning, translation assistance, and educational practice.
- Personal and business use consistent with applicable law.

## Prohibited use

Users must not use the service to:

1. Violate laws or rights of others.
2. Generate or distribute harmful, abusive, exploitative, or deceptive content.
3. Facilitate fraud, malware, or unauthorized access to systems/data.
4. Produce content for harassment, discrimination, or violence.
5. Bypass safety controls, access controls, or policy enforcement mechanisms.
6. Use the platform in any way prohibited by underlying model provider terms.

## Enforcement

We may remove content, limit features, suspend accounts, or terminate access for
policy violations.

## Reporting

Report abuse to: `abuse@yourdomain.com`

## Notes

This template must be reviewed by counsel before production use.
